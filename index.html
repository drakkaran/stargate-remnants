Stargate: Remnants — Help

Objective

Your base woke from cryo-sleep to find Earth's gate address won't lock. Rebuild, research, and explore other worlds to discover what happened — and find a way to Pegasus.

Resources

Naquadah, Food, Power, and Data are your primary resources. Manage them carefully. Personnel consume 1 Food per day; each installed room consumes 1 Power per day.

Base Building

Build rooms to expand capabilities. Gate Room and Control Room are active by default. Construction takes a few in-game hours and consumes resources.

Personnel

Each team member has Combat, Diplomacy, Science, and Survival stats. Send teams off-world on missions. Match personnel to mission types for better success chances.

Gate Travel

Dial known gate addresses to visit other worlds. Decode new addresses by solving riddles, gathering intelligence, or using Ancient Database technology.

Research

Build a Research Lab to access the tech tree. Research unlocks new buildings, upgrades, and defences. Some rare technologies are only found off-world.

Time

Each game hour is 5 real-world seconds. The day/night bar at the top tracks the current time. Events and consumption happen each tick.
